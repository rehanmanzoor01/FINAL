Why Nundresh

Built for Kashmir's
apple growers.

We don't just store apples — we preserve livelihoods. Every chamber, every sensor, every process is designed around Kashmir's unique climate, harvest cycles, and export demands.

❄️
Controlled Atmosphere (CA) StorageOxygen-reduced chambers extend shelf life by 4–6 months — keeping apples market-fresh longer.
🌡️
Precision Temperature ManagementIndustrial sensors maintain ±0.5°C accuracy. Real-time alerts and SMS notifications for every deviation.
📊
Digital Tracking & ReportingEvery consignment tagged, logged, and traceable. Transparent condition reports on request.
⚡
Zero Downtime GuaranteeDual-circuit power with auto-switching diesel backup. Sub-30-second failover ensures continuous operation.
Our Services

End-to-end apple care.

From harvest intake to export dispatch — every step managed with precision and care.

How It Works

Simple. Reliable. Efficient.

01
Intake & Inspection

Apples received, weighed, and visually inspected. Each consignment logged with unique ID.

02
Pre-Cooling

Rapid field-heat removal in pre-cooling chambers before main storage placement.

03
Chamber Storage

Allocated to regular or CA chamber based on variety and required storage duration.

04
Monitoring

24/7 sensor monitoring with automated alerts and periodic condition reports.

05
Dispatch

Grading, packing, and logistics coordination at your chosen time. Documentation ready.
